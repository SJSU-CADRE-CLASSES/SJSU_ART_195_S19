3rR0r

Emily and Nicole


Glitch (/gli-tch/) - a technological hiccup in the system that temporarily malfunctions in a device. As digital media arts students, we have all experienced errors with our code, heard laptop fans vigorously spinning in the middle of a render, gone through multiple versions of a project before settling on the right one, or even Adobe Illustrator crashing right when we were about to save our files. For this piece, we decided to go with glitch art and the way it aesthetically portrays the trials and errors we go through.

We decided to build our installation in pillars built from white boxes to depict the idea of walking through the glitches in a userâ€™s device. We created glitch animations from After Effects that portrayed how fascinating and appealing flaws can be. There are many errors that we go through as digital students, along with the many glitches that we experience, and a whole lot of frustration, but with the blood, sweat, and tears, we are able to generate something so beautiful.
