My Baby World VR

Artist Statement

Don M Vo

As an artist, I want to use VR to warp the perspectives of the viewers to replace their views in different worlds. My Baby World VR places the perception in a shrunken down size of a baby. Technically, the viewer becomes the baby and experiences life in the eyes of a baby again. Often people grow up and stay in the adult point of view for majority of their human lives. We do not recall how much bigger the human world really is. That time span is just too short to recollect as our minds were in an infant state processing the world in a rapid pace. This VR immersive setup will be a throw back into the baby state with our adult mindset to process.

Using the Autodesk Maya and Unity software, I remodeled and modeled all assets from scratch in my image. Using my childhood home as a base, I rebuilt it to truly show what it was like to be a baby version of me. I tried to recreate or create every furniture, walls, or object within my memory of how it was. Every 3D model is made as low-poly and recognizable as possible. The rendered scenes should be as close to reality as possible to enhance the viewerâ€™s perception of realism. Lastly, the 3D printed baby was just for prop purposes as the viewers are seeing the VR simulation in its eyes.
